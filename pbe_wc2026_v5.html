① PBE — Predictive Bayesian Ensemble

PBE = 0.35×Prior + 0.65×Likelihood + StageBonus
Prior = ConfStr × HistPrestige + CoachBonus
Likelihood = 0.30×Goals + 0.25×Defense + 0.20×Poss + 0.25×Efficiency

Bayesian update: prior is the pre-tournament belief; likelihood is in-tournament evidence. Evidence weight grows with matches played (caps at 65%). Stage bonus rewards points earned.

② ELO — FIFA-style Elo Rating

E = 1 / (1 + 10^((Rb−Ra)/400))
Ra' = Ra + K × (S − E)
K = 60 (WC), goal_weight = 1 + ln(1 + |GD|)

Classic chess-derived rating adapted for football. K=60 for World Cup matches. Goal difference inflates K via logarithmic multiplier. Win probability derived from rating difference.

③ POI — Independent Poisson Goal Model

λH = avgGoals × AttH × DefA × homeAdv
λA = avgGoals × AttA × DefH
P(score) = e^(−λ) × λ^k / k!
Simulate 0–6 goals each team

Each team's expected goals modelled as independent Poisson processes. Attack/defense coefficients derived from pre-tournament historical scoring rates. Result probabilities from full score-matrix simulation.

④ MKT — Market-Implied Probability

P_mkt(W) = BaseOdds × FormMod × RankMod
FormMod = 0.5 + 0.5×(recentW/recentM)
RankMod = 1 − 0.3×(FIFARank_gap/100)

Replicates bookmaker consensus using FIFA ranking differentials and recent form (last 5 qualifying matches). Normalised to sum to 1 after margin removal. Most predictive for short-term form.

★ ENS — Integrated Ensemble

ENS = 0.30×PBE + 0.25×ELO + 0.25×POI + 0.20×MKT

Weighted combination. PBE gets highest weight (tournament-specific Bayesian evidence). ELO and Poisson equal weight (complementary structural models). Market gets 20% (useful signal but may lag upsets). All weights sum to 1. Score probabilities averaged across models with same weighting.

⚠️ Illustrative heuristic — not a calibrated statistical forecast. Pre-tournament baseline frozen at Jun 10 2026; in-tournament recalibration applied after each result via PBE and ELO updates only.
